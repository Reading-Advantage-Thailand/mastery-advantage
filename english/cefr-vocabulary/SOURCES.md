## B2 First / FCE Evidence

Cambridge does not publish a vocabulary list for B2 First or B2 First for
Schools. Its candidate guidance states that exams at CEFR B2 or above do not
have particular language specifications or vocabulary lists.

## CEFR And Reference Level Descriptions

- Council of Europe CEFR level descriptions:
  <https://www.coe.int/en/web/common-european-framework-reference-languages/level-descriptions>
- Council of Europe Reference Level Descriptions:
  <https://www.coe.int/en/web/common-european-framework-reference-languages/reference-level-descriptions>

CEFR descriptors are language-independent. English vocabulary aligned to CEFR
must come from an English-specific Reference Level Description or another
documented lexical source.

## English Vocabulary In Use

Cambridge publishes the frontmatter and complete index for each current
Vocabulary in Use level. The frontmatter supplies unit titles and the index maps
lexical entries to unit numbers.
